Instructions

All the entries should be filled according to the rules given below:-

  • There is no age restrictions.

  • Sex : Use numerical 1 or 2 (1 = Male and 2 = Female).

  • chest pain type : (1 = typical angina , 2= atypical angina , 3= non-anginal pain , 4 = asymptomatic).

  • Resting blood pressure : resting blood pressure (in mm Hg on admission to hospital ).

  • cholesterol : serum cholesterol in mg/dl.

  • Blood sugar level : ( fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl) (1 = true , 0 =false).

  • Exercise induced angina : ( 0 = yes , 1 = no).
